  - title: Ikan Bakar Lunch
    text: |
      I made my way to my next food spot, which serves ikan bakar or grilled fish. This place wrapped the fish in a banana leaf with a spice paste and cooked it on a hot griddle. It's then served up with a spicy dipping sauce, rice, and veggies. I asked which fish had the fewest bones since I don't like dealing with bones and was recommended pari aka stingray! That was something I'd never tried before so double bonus - fewer bones and something new!
