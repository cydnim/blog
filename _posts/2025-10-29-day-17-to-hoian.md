  - title: Tough Decisions
    text: |
      Our area around the beach was extremely wet, and the Hoi An ancient town (which is a unesco cultural heritage due to it being a very well-preserved trading port) was completely flooded, meaning we wouldn't be able to visit it at all. So the two things we were looking forward to (beach relaxation and old town exploration) were impossible. As a result, we ended up deciding to change our tickets onward to Ho Chi Minh City - instead of leaving on the 31st at 5pm, we cut our stay short to leave tomorrow (on the 30th) at 2pm in order to have more time there.
