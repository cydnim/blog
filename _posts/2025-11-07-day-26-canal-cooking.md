  - title: Cooking Class 2
    text: |
      I was very excited for this class and it lived up to expectations! I had specifically asked for something spicy, and not the usual pad thai/green curry that literally every other cooking class seemed to offer and my teacher Jib delivered! She welcomed me into her family kitchen and helped me cook three dishes which were mostly new to me: yum tua plu (spicy wing bean ‚Äúsalad‚Äù), tod mun moo (spiced pork fritters) and pad cha (spicy stir fried meat). The idea behind pad cha is you come home drunk and throw whatever you have in the fridge into a wok and fry it up. If you add noodles to it you get pad kee mao, aka drunken noodles!
