  - title: Candlenut
    text: |
      Slightly sweaty from my speedwalk through the garden, I made it to Candlenut in time to meet Steven and Paul for our reservation. This was a fantastic meal, and certainly lived up to its michelin star. Candlenut focuses on Peranakan cuisine. Peranakan is a word used to refer to the descendants of early Chinese migrants to South East Asia, and the cuisine combines Chinese, Malay, Javanese and South Indian influences for some delicious food. Make sure to click into each photo for a description of the dish and what I thought of it!
