  - title: "To Da Nang and HCMC"
    text: |
      Our travel to HCMC had a couple delays - first our driver was 15 min late due to the flooding, and then the plane was just stuck in place for over an hour after everyone had boarded. We ended up leaving Da Nang after the point where we were meant to reach HCMC! After arriving in HCMC, we took a cab to our hotel for the night, checked in and then headed out to eat.
  - title: "Ban Xeo et al."
    text: |
      We walked 20ish min from our hotel to Bep Me In, a family-style Vietnamese restaurant that also offered some street food dishes. A great first meal in HCMC.

It's a crispy vietnamese crepe made of a rice flour batter (it isn't an omelette! the yellow is from turmeric) folded in half around a filling of stir fried beansprouts and your choice of protein. We asked how to eat it and were told to take a piece of rice paper, place a piece of lettuce in, followed by a slice of banh xeo and some of the other greens, and eat it kind of like a taco. Super tasty and the highlight of this restaurant for me."
