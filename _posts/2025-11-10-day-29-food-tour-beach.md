    text: |
      When I got hungry, I headed over to a restaurant my food tour guide had recommended to try khao kluk kapi, which is shrimp paste fried rice surrounded by various toppings. I got it with chicken. It was super tasty, the fried rice was super savoury and mixing in the toppings bit by bit gave it a lot of variety in taste and texture. Would recommend.
