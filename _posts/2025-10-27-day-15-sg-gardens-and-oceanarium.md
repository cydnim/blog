  - title: Gardens by The Bay
    text: |
      After lunch we walked over to the Gardens by the Bay. They have two huge conservatories there that are actually air conditioned in order to allow plants that need a cooler environment to survive in Singapore's heat. They use some specially treated glass to minimize heat passage while maximizing light for the plants in addition to some other strategies to make these large air conditioned domes more sustainable. We also went up to the top of one of the "supertree"s, which are large vertical gardens made to look like trees.
